About Me

My name is Rodolfo Azevedo Soares, a former player and currently a goalkeeper coach. I spent 6 years as an athlete, both in youth and professional levels. Achievements, titles, defeats, injuries, periods of glory, and struggle have shaped my character and professionalism.

I carry every second earned at Botafogo - RJ, América - RJ, and in the Brazilian National Team's youth setup. These clubs and structures taught me the best of football, the ideal path to become a reference athlete, and today I pass on the same knowledge to my athletes.

In football, we find smiles in victories, pain in defeats, but above all, we find passion and dreams. That's what I work for, it's in dreams that I believe. If all this ends, if all the magic of football ceases to exist, WHEN IT'S JUST FOOTBALL, I'LL STOP!

Training Philosophy

My training is based on a comprehensive approach that combines technical improvement, physical development, and mental strengthening. I believe that the goalkeeper is the last line of defense and, therefore, must be the most prepared person on the field.

I train my goalkeepers to always be confident and happy on the field. That's why the training sessions are strong, fast, intense, and with high volume, preparing the athlete for big games and large audiences. The pressure in training is part of psychological development, taking the athlete out of their comfort zone and forcing them to make confident decisions, whether with the ball at their feet or during saves in the game.
